# 🌍 Carbon Compass

**Carbon Compass** is an interactive, privacy-first carbon footprint awareness platform designed to help individuals estimate, track, and reduce their environmental impact using the power of Google tools and AI.

By combining real-time client-side calculations with personalized recommendations, Carbon Compass empowers users to turn awareness into tangible climate action.

## 🚀 Key Features

* **Real-time Carbon Calculator:** Estimates monthly CO₂e emissions across four core categories: Home Energy, Transport, Food, and Consumption.
* **Personalized AI Insights:** Analyzes footprint data, household size, commute habits, and sustainability goals to rank impact areas and suggest tailored next steps.
* **12+ Google Tool Integrations:** Instantly connects insights to actions by generating pre-filled events, transit routes, shareable action plans, and search queries across Google Maps, Calendar, Gmail, Sheets, Drive, Shopping, Earth, Trends, Scholar, Docs, Keep, and YouTube.
* **Progress Tracking:** Tracks personal reduction pledges and historical assessments locally in the browser with no tracking or server overhead.
* **Inclusive & Secure Design:** Built from the ground up for security and accessibility (WCAG AA compliant), keeping all personal data completely local to the device.

## 🌟 Google Tool Integrations

* **Google Maps:** Eco-routing transit directions between your home and work destination.
* **Google Calendar:** Scheduled monthly carbon reviews to stay accountable.
* **Gmail:** Shared carbon reduction action plans to inspire family and friends.
* **Google Sheets & Google Drive:** Formatted data exports for tracking progress and backing up histories.
* **Gemini AI:** Personalized AI prompts tailored to your top emission categories.
* **Google Shopping & Search:** Discovering eco-friendly products and reduction tips.
* **Google Earth & Trends:** Visualizing global climate impacts and sustainability public interest.
* **Google Keep & Nest:** Shared checklists and smart home routines.
