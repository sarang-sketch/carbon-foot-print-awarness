    Daily Eco Tips

    Small changes, big impact

    🚿

    Shorter Showers

    Reducing shower time by 2 minutes saves ~10 gallons of water and the energy to heat it.

    🔌

    Unplug Standby

    Standby power accounts for 5-10% of residential energy use. Use smart power strips.

    🥦

    Meatless Monday

    One plant-based day per week can reduce your food carbon footprint by ~15%.

    🚲

    Bike Short Trips

    50% of car trips are under 5 km. Cycling these saves ~0.9 kg CO₂ per trip.

    🌡️

    Smart Thermostat

    Adjusting temp 1°C saves about 7% on heating bills and significantly reduces emissions.

    🛍️

    Buy Secondhand

    Secondhand clothing reduces carbon footprint by ~82% compared to buying new.

    💧

    Cold Water Wash

    Washing clothes in cold water can reduce the carbon footprint of laundry by 50%.

    📱

    Digital Declutter

    Delete unused emails, photos, and files to reduce cloud storage energy consumption.

    Smart Carbon Insights

    Our decision engine analyzes your input context to generate personalized, actionable intelligence.

    🧠

    Pattern Recognition

    Identifies your highest-impact emission category and ranks all four areas (home, transport, food, consumption) to prioritize the most effective reductions.

    🎯

    Context-Aware Recommendations

    Adapts action plans based on your commute mode, household size, and sustainability goal. "Save money" triggers cost-saving tips; "net zero" suggests deeper changes.

    ⚡

    Dynamic Scoring

    Classifies your footprint into three tiers — Planet Protector (≤350 kg), Carbon Climber (≤800 kg), and High Impact (>800 kg) — with tailored messaging for each.

    🔗

    Google Tool Matching

    Automatically maps your top emission category to the most relevant Google tool. Transport → Maps, Home → Nest, Food → Search, Consumption → Shopping.

    🤖

    Gemini Prompt Engineering

    Generates optimized prompts for Google's Gemini AI that include your total footprint, top category, and constraints (low cost, 7-day timeline, no guilt).

      Code Quality

      Modular ES modules, pure functions, frozen constants, JSDoc comments, semantic naming, no framework dependencies, and repository under 10 MB.

      Security

      HTML sanitization, URL encoding, CSV formula injection prevention, no API keys exposed, no server-side code, Content Security Policy ready.

      Efficiency

      Static assets only, zero runtime dependencies, instant client-side calculations, debounced inputs, lazy-loaded sections, minimal DOM manipulation.

      Testing

      Node.js native test runner, unit tests for all core functions, edge case coverage, integration tests for Google URL generation and CSV export.

      Accessibility

      WCAG 2.1 AA compliant: semantic landmarks, ARIA labels, keyboard navigation, skip links, focus indicators, live regions, reduced motion support, print stylesheet.
